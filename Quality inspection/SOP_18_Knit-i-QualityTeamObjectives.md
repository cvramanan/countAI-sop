# Quality Team Objectives (ISO 9001:2015)

**Doc Name:** KNITI-SOP-QC-00  
**Company:** COUNTAI  
**Revision No:** R00

---

## 1. Primary Quality Objective

Ensure all products meet defined quality standards and customer requirements through effective inspection, testing, and continuous improvement.

---

## 2. Quality Objective Monitoring Table (ISO Required)

| Objective | Target | Frequency | Responsible | Record |
|------------|---------|------------|--------------|----------|
| Reduce failure rate | ≤ 2% | Monthly | QA Engineer | Failure Log |
| Improve FPY | ≥ 98% | Monthly | QC Team | Test Report |
| Zero defective dispatch | 100% | Daily | QC Inspector | Dispatch Report |
| Reduce complaints | ≤ 2 / month | Monthly | QA Manager | Complaint Log |
| CAPA closure | ≤ 7 days | Weekly | QA Engineer | CAPA Log |
| Supplier quality | ≤ 3% rejection | Monthly | QC Team | MRIR |
| Internal audit | 100% completion | Every 6 months | QA Manager | Audit Report |

---

## 3. Measurable Quality Objectives (Mandatory for ISO Audit)

ISO 9001:2015 requires measurable objectives with defined targets and monitoring mechanisms.

---

### Objective 1: Reduce Product Failure Rate

**Goal:**  
Reduce internal product failure rate.

**Target:**  
Failure rate ≤ 2%

**Measurement Method:**  
Daily Failure Log, QC Records

**Monitoring Frequency:**  
Monthly

**Responsible:**  
Quality Engineer

---

### Objective 2: Improve First Pass Yield (FPY)

**Goal:**  
Ensure products pass testing on the first attempt.

**Target:**  
FPY ≥ 98%

**Measurement Method:**  
Cabinet Test Reports

**Monitoring Frequency:**  
Monthly

**Responsible:**  
Quality Team

---

### Objective 3: Zero Dispatch of Defective Products

**Goal:**  
Prevent defective products from reaching customers.

**Target:**  
100% QC approval before dispatch

**Measurement Method:**  
Dispatch Clearance Report

**Monitoring Frequency:**  
Daily

**Responsible:**  
QC Inspector

---

### Objective 4: Reduce Customer Complaints

**Goal:**  
Minimize field failures and customer complaints.

**Target:**  
≤ 2 complaints per month

**Measurement Method:**  
Customer Complaint Log

**Monitoring Frequency:**  
Monthly

**Responsible:**  
Quality Manager

---

### Objective 5: Corrective Action Closure Time

**Goal:**  
Resolve quality issues quickly.

**Target:**  
CAPA closure within 7 days

**Measurement Method:**  
CAPA Log

**Monitoring Frequency:**  
Weekly

**Responsible:**  
Quality Engineer

---

### Objective 6: Incoming Material Quality Improvement

**Goal:**  
Ensure supplier material quality.

**Target:**  
Incoming rejection rate ≤ 3%

**Measurement Method:**  
MRIR Report

**Monitoring Frequency:**  
Monthly

**Responsible:**  
Quality Team

---

### Objective 7: Internal Audit Compliance

**Goal:**  
Ensure full compliance with ISO procedures.

**Target:**  
100% internal audit completion as planned

**Measurement Method:**  
Internal Audit Report

**Monitoring Frequency:**  
Every 6 months

**Responsible:**  
Quality Manager
