# 1. Objective

To ensure smooth, documented travel coordination and on-time engineer arrival at site — with **zero installation delays caused by travel issues**.

### Key SLAs

- Travel confirmation: **Minimum 48 hours before departure**
- Customer notification: **Minimum 24 hours before departure**
- Ticket booking: **Minimum 48 hours before travel**
- Arrival reporting: **Within 1 hour of site arrival**

---

# 2. Scope

Applicable for all **installation and service visits** — both **domestic and international**.

---

# 3. Roles & Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|---------------|
| Project Manager | Confirms installation readiness and approves deputation |
| Service Manager | Approves installation date and reviews travel plan |
| Service Manager/ PM | Books tickets and shares travel details |
| Field Engineer | Confirms travel details and reports arrival |

---

# 4. Travel Confirmation  
**SLA: Minimum 48 Hours Before Departure**

1. Confirm installation readiness with Sales / Customer (**written confirmation required**)
2. Verify all pre-requisites completed (ref: Pre-Requisites SOP) — **100% mandatory**
3. Confirm installation date with Service Manager approval (**no verbal confirmation**)
4. Send customer notification email including:
   - Engineer name  
   - Dispatch date  
   - Arrival date & time  
   - Accommodation support  

**Customer Notification SLA:** Minimum **24 hours before departure**

---

# 5. Ticket Booking  
**SLA: Minimum 48 Hours Before Travel**

1. Book train / flight tickets based on confirmed schedule  
2. Arrange local transport (arrival point → customer site)  
3. Share travel details (**PDF or offline-accessible file**) with:
   - Service Manager  
   - Project Manager  
   - Internal team  
4. For international visits:
   - Carry physical copies of tickets and all supporting documents  
5. Ensure engineer acknowledges ticket details before departure  

---

# 6. Site Arrival  
**SLA: Report Within 1 Hour**

1. Inform customer upon arrival (**call or WhatsApp only — no email**)  
2. Report arrival to internal team including:
   - Arrival time  
   - Site name     
3. If delayed:
   - Inform Service Manager & customer immediately  
   - Share revised ETA  

---

# 7. KPIs & Success Criteria

| Metric | Target |
|------|------|
| Travel confirmation completed | ≥48 hours before departure — 100% |
| Customer notification sent | ≥24 hours before departure — 100% |
| Tickets booked | ≥48 hours before travel — 100% |
| Engineer arrival on schedule | 100% |
| Arrival reporting | Within 1 hour — 100% |
| Travel-related installation delays | 0 |

---

# 8. Confirmation Checklist

- [ ] Installation readiness confirmed in writing

- [ ] Installation date approved by Service Manager  
- [ ] Customer notified ≥24 hours before departure
