# KNIT-I Engineer Deputation  
## Travel Planning & Site Visit Management Guidelines  

---

## 1. Objective
To ensure smooth travel coordination and timely engineer arrival at site.

---

## 2. Scope
Applicable for all installation and service visits.

---

## 3. Travel Confirmation

- Confirm installation readiness with Sales / Customers.  
- Verify pre-requisites completed (Verify Pre-requisites Checklist).  
- Confirm installation date with Service Manager approval.  
- Mail the customer about engineer dispatch including:
  - Dispatch date  
  - Expected arrival time  
  - Travel / accommodation support required  

---

## 4. Ticket Booking

- Book train / flight tickets.  
- Arrange local transport.  
- Share travel details internally with concerned team members.

---

## 5. Ticket Handling

- Ticket must be shared via **PDF** or offline-accessible file.  
- Engineer must download ticket for offline access.  

### For International Visits:
- Carry physical copy of flight tickets.  
- Carry all supporting travel documents as physical copies.  

---

## 6. Site Arrival

- Inform customer immediately upon arrival.  
- Report arrival status to internal team.  

---

## 7. Acceptance Criteria

- Engineer reaches site as scheduled.  
- No travel-related installation delay.  

---

# Confirmation Checklist

- [ ] Installation date confirmed  
- [ ] Pre-requisites validated  
- [ ] Tickets booked  
- [ ] Travel details shared  
- [ ] Arrival confirmed
