# KNIT-I Pre-Requisites
## Installation Requirements & Site Preparation Guidelines

---

## 1. Internet Requirements

- Individual LAN connection required for each machine.
- Minimum > 10 Mbps (Upload & Download speed must be stable and equal) per machine.
- Local permanent IP required for each machine.(recomended)
- Dedicated internet line recommended (avoid shared heavy-traffic networks).

---

## 2. Fabric Conditions

- Maintain reasonable winding tension for clear defect visibility.
- Defects must be clearly visible while fabric is undisturbed on the machine.
- If the machine is configured for OW / TUB condiition - it should not be changed in the machine lifetime
- Cloth fold/ fabric waviness should not be present in the machine

---

## 3. Machine Adjustments & Mechanical Requirements

- Minor adjustments in fabric spreader may be required to increase the defect visibility.
- Fan / Air rod adjustment may be required for top camera mounting.
- Tubular machines may require small door holes for camera mounting.
- Space required for:
  - Cabinet mounting
  - Small machine hole for mounting brackets
- Fabric Ring must be available to:
  - Maintain even camera distance
- Camera cable routing will be done from the outside part of the machine.
- Machine parts must be symmetric.
- Wobbling must be avoided.
- Basic counter sensor signals required from knitting machines.
- RIB-Tubular machines:
  - Internal light may need to be removed for top camera installation.

---

## 4. Power Supply Requirements

- Reliable 230V (+ or - 5 v)  AC power supply (or) Dedicated 10A plug point required.
- Stabilizer recommended.
- 1 KVA UPS with approximately 15 minutes backup recommended.
- Proper earthing required:
  - Independent earth & neutral connections.
  - Earth-to-neutral voltage difference < 1V.
  - Stable earth continuity.
- Separate phase, neutral, and ground wiring mandatory.
- Space required to mount:
  - MCB
  - SMPS

---

## 5. Support & Operational Requirements

- Electrician must be available during installation.
- Machine technician must be available during installation.
- Permission required to intentionally create defects for AI training.
- Dedicated operator must be assigned for KNIT-I training.
- KNIT-I team must be informed in advance about:
  - Fabric style changes
  - GSM changes
  - Color changes
  - Structure changes
    
## 6. Pre requisite - validation:
- validate and confirm woth customer whether the arrange has been done before booking the engineer travel tickets with service manager in loop

---

## Confirmation Checklist Before Installation

- [ ] Dedicated LAN connection available
- [ ] Required internet speed verified (>10 Mbps)
- [ ] Optional - Permanent local IP configured
- [ ] Fabric tension properly maintained
- [ ] Cabinet mounting space prepared
- [ ] Proper earthing verified (<1V difference)
- [ ] UPS installed and functional
- [ ] Technician & electrician available
- [ ] Operator assigned for training
- [ ] Permission given for defect creation
