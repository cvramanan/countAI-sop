# SOP: Sales Order Handover to Production

---

## 1. Purpose
To ensure all sales orders are accurately transferred to the Production team along with complete technical and commercial details, enabling smooth production planning and execution.

---

## 2. Scope
This SOP applies to all Sales, Application/Technical, and Production teams involved in order processing.

---

## 3. Responsibility

**Sales Person**
- Collect and submit complete Technical Data Sheet (TDS) and Sales Order.

**Technical/Application Team**
- Verify technical feasibility (if applicable).

**Production Team**
- Review and accept order for production.

**Planning/Coordinator**
- Maintain records and track handover.

---

## 4. Procedure

### 4.1 Sales Order Preparation
- Sales person receives confirmed Purchase Order (PO) from customer.  
- Sales person creates the internal Sales Order.  
- Sales person downloads and fills the Technical Data Sheet (TDS) in the prescribed format.

---

---

### 4.3 Internal Verification
- Sales person reviews TDS for completeness.  
- Any missing information must be obtained from the customer before handover.

---

### 4.4 Handover to Production

Sales person submits the following to Production:

- ✅ Completed TDS (as per format)  
- ✅ All required machine images  
- ✅ Customer special requirements (if any)

**Submission Mode:**  
- Email (as per company practice)

---

### 4.5 Production Acknowledgement
- Production reviews documents within **2 hours**.  
- If details are complete → **Order accepted**.  
- If gaps found → **Query raised to Sales**.  
- Planning team updates the production schedule.

---

### 4.6 Record Keeping
- All SO + TDS files must be stored in the project folder.  
- Version control must be maintained.  
- Any later change must follow formal revision.

---

## 5. Checklist for Sales (Quick Reference)

Before handover, confirm:

- [ ] Sales Order created  
- [ ] TDS fully filled  
- [ ] Images attached  
- [ ] Technical review done (if required)  
- [ ] Customer special notes added  
- [ ] Files uploaded/shared
