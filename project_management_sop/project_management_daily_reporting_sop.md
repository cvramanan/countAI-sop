## 1. Purpose
The purpose of this SOP is to ensure **daily visibility** into project execution, progress, risks, and delivery health for leadership and stakeholders.

This email acts as the **single source of truth** for daily project status.

---

## 2. Reporting Responsibility
- The **Project Manager** is solely responsible for:
  - Collecting daily updates from the team
  - Ensuring Jira reflects the current state
  - Sending the report on time and accurately

> This responsibility cannot be delegated.

---

## 3. Email Schedule
- **When:** End of Day (EOD)
- **Days:** All working days
- **Expectation:** Sent without reminders

---

## 4. Email Recipients
- **To:** CTO, CEO  
- **CC:** Relevant stakeholders (as defined for the project)

No additional recipients without prior approval.

---
