# SOP_002_NEW DEVELOPER ONBOARDING & KNOWLEDGE TRANSFER
**Version:** 1.0  
**Owner:** Project Manager  
**Applies To:** All Engineering Hires (Full-time / Contract / Intern)

---

## 1. Purpose
This SOP ensures:

- Zero chaos onboarding  
- Controlled & traceable system access  
- Fast productivity (reduce *Time to First PR*)  
- Structured Knowledge Transfer (KT)  
- Security & compliance alignment  
- Clear ownership & accountability  

**Goal: A new developer should:**

- Push their first PR within 5 working days  
- Fully understand product architecture within 2 weeks  
- Work independently within 30 days  

---

## 2. Guiding Principles

- Access is role-based, not person-based  
- No undocumented knowledge transfer  
- No production access without formal approval  
- Onboarding is a measurable engineering process  
- Every new hire must produce output within Week 1  

---
