# Pre-Installation Material Auditing 
## Standard Operating Procedure

---

# 1. Purpose

To ensure safe, verified, and standardized installation of the KNIT-I system at customer locations.

This SOP must be followed strictly for every installation.

---

# 2. Pre-Installation – Delivery Verification

## Step 1: Delivery Check

- Contact **Mr. Churchil** and get delivery update.
- Confirm whether the package has been received.

### If Package Not Received:
- Follow up with logistics.
- Do not proceed further.

---

## Step 2: Inspect Outer Packaging

- Inspect outer packaging for visible damage.

### If Damaged:
- Raise complaint to **count.ai**
- Document damage with photos.
- Stop installation until resolved.

### If Fine:
- Proceed to unpacking.

---

# 3. Cabinet Inspection

## Step 3: Open and Inspect

- Carefully open package.
- Inspect cabinets inside.

### If Internal Damage Found:
- Raise complaint immediately.
- Stop installation.

### If All Good:
- Proceed to electrical verification.

---

# 4. Electrical Verification (Customer Side)

## Step 4: Verify Voltages

Measure and confirm:

- Phase to Neutral Voltage  
- Neutral to Earth Voltage  
- Earth to Body Voltage  

⚠ Maintain stable neutral-to-earth voltage for at least **1 minute**.

### If Voltage Unstable:
- Stop installation.
- Inform customer electrician.

### If Voltage Stable:
- Proceed.
---

# 5. Inspect Cabinet Components

## Step 6: Mechanical & Wiring Check

### Verify:

- All fittings are securely installed.
- Wire and cable connections are tight and neat.
- M12 connectors are properly plugged in.

### Check for Physical Damage:

- Cracks
- Deformation
- Exposed conductors

---

### If Any Issue Found:
- Troubleshoot
- Fix the issue
- Reinspect
- Proceed only after confirmation

---

# 6. SMPS Voltage Confirmation

## Step 7: Confirm SMPS Input Voltage Setting

- Ensure SMPS input voltage setting is **230V**.

⚠ If SMPS is set to 115V:
- Adjust immediately to 230V.
- Failure to do so may cause permanent damage.

---


# 7. Final Checklist Review

- Review complete installation checklist.
- Record results.

---

# 8. Completion Decision

### If All Checks Passed:
- Complete installation.
- Mark status as **Pre-Installation Check Complete**.

### If Any Error Occurs:
- Troubleshoot and fix the issue.
- Do not close installation until fully resolved.

---

# 9. Installation Closure Criteria

Installation is considered complete only when:

- Packaging inspection completed  
- Electrical verification stable  
- MCB cutoff confirmed ≤ 240V  
- All cabinet fittings secure  
- No physical damage found  
- SMPS set to 230V  
- Checklist recorded  
- No unresolved errors  

---

# 10. Compliance Note

- Installation must not proceed under unstable voltage conditions.
- Installation must not be completed without full inspection.
- Any deviation must be documented and approved.
