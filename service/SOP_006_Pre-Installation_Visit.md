## 6. Cabinet Mounting Placement  

### Procedure:
1. Identify safe mounting location near machine.
2. Ensure:
   - No vibration
   - Protection from oil/water
   - Accessible for operator
3. Measure distances:
   - Machine to cabinet
   - Cabinet to power source
   - Cabinet to display location
