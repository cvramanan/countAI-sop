# KNIT-I TRAINING COMPLETION – MANAGEMENT ACKNOWLEDGEMENT  
## Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)

---
## Engineer Notes

This SOP **must be followed for every installation**.

- Mandatory for new installations  
- Mandatory even if installation is done in the same mill for the second time  
- No installation shall be closed without management acknowledgement 

# 1. Purpose

To ensure that every KNIT-I installation is followed by structured training and formal management acknowledgement.

This SOP is **mandatory** and must be followed:

- For every new installation  
- For repeat installations in the same mill  
- Without exception  

Training is not considered complete without documented acknowledgement.

---

# 2. Responsibility

The **Installation Engineer / Trainer** is fully responsible for:

- Conducting complete structured training  
- Ensuring all required personnel attend  
- Verifying understanding of key functions  
- Collecting required signatures  
- Submitting the signed document to company management  

Failure to follow this SOP will result in installation closure being withheld.

---

# 3. Procedure – Mandatory Steps

---

## Step 1: Fill Basic Installation Details

Before starting training, the trainer must complete the following:

- Company / Mill Name  
- Customer Location  
- Machine Name  
- Training Conducted By  
- Training Date(s)  


---

## Step 2: Conduct Machine Training (Mandatory)

The trainer must cover:

### 2.1 Machine Operation
- KNIT-I machine operation & usage  
- Roll management process  
- Alarm authentication  
- System navigation  

### 2.2 System Workflow
- Standard workflow procedure  
- Best operating practices  
- Data accuracy importance  

### 2.3 Troubleshooting Procedures

The trainer must demonstrate:

- Camera focus recalibration  
- Wire re-routing  
- Exposure & gain adjustment  
- Basic fault diagnosis  
- Recovery procedures  
- Bypass protocol (when applicable)  

> Live demonstration is mandatory wherever applicable.

---

## Step 3: Cover Required Personnel – Machine Training

The following personnel must attend:

- Operators  
- Foreman / Shift In-Charge  
- Data Entry Operators  
- Quality Control Team  

Actions required:

- Record names of all attendees clearly  
- Ensure active participation  
- Provide hands-on demonstration  

> If any category is absent, reschedule and complete training before installation closure.

---

## Step 4: Conduct Dashboard Training (Mandatory for Management)

A separate session must be conducted for:

- Knitting Manager  
- General Manager  
- Vice President  

The following must be explained:

- Dashboard usage  
- Reports generation  
- Monitoring process  
- Data interpretation  
- Performance tracking  

> Management training cannot be skipped.

---

## Step 5: Confirm Understanding

Before collecting signatures, the trainer must:

- Ask validation questions  
- Ensure team can operate basic functions independently  
- Confirm ability to respond to alarms  
- Demonstrate troubleshooting capability  

> Do NOT collect acknowledgement without validation.

---

## Step 6: Obtain Management Acknowledgement

After successful completion of training:

1. Present the acknowledgement statement.
2. Explain that training has been completed.
3. Obtain signature from authorized management representative.

The following must be collected:

- Name  
- Designation  
- Signature  
- Date  
- Company Seal  

> ⚠ This step is mandatory.  
> Training is NOT considered complete without signed acknowledgement.

---

## Step 7: Trainer Confirmation

The trainer must sign the document:

- Trainer Name  
- Trainer Signature  
- Date  

By signing, the trainer confirms:

- Training was conducted completely  
- All required personnel were covered  
- SOP was followed fully  
- No step was skipped  

---

# 4. Important Compliance Note

This SOP must be followed for **every installation**.

- Even if installation is done in the same mill for the second time, training must be repeated.  
- No installation shall be closed without signed acknowledgement.  
- No exceptions are permitted.  

---

# 5. Record Retention

The signed acknowledgement must be:

- Submitted to company management  
- Stored in installation records  
- Digitally archived if applicable  

Retention period: As per company policy.
