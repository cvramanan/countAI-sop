# 1. Objective

This SOP ensures effective communication and structured support following KNITI product installation at customer sites.

Creating an official WhatsApp group enables:
- Immediate coordination
- Rapid issue resolution
- Continuous system validation

---

# 2. Scope

This procedure applies to **all new KNITI installations** and must be completed on the **same day as installation**.

---

# 3. Responsibilities

## Installation Engineer

- Create WhatsApp group immediately after installation
- Add all mandatory stakeholders within 1 hour
- Send installation confirmation message
- Introduce Customer Support team
- Introduce KNITI Responsible Person
- Document group creation in installation checklist

## Operations Manager & Service Team Lead

- Monitor group communications
- Ensure SLA response compliance
- Escalate unresolved issues

## KNITI Responsible Person

- Primary technical contact
- Provide support guidance
- Facilitate communication between teams

---

# 4. WhatsApp Group Creation Procedure

---

## 4.1 Group Naming Convention

**Format:**
[Customer Company Name] – KNITI

**Examples:**

- ABC Textiles – KNITI  
- XYZ Manufacturing – KNITI  
- Global Fabric Ltd – KNITI  

### Requirements

- Use official customer company name
- Include separator “–”
- Add “KNITI” identifier
- Maintain naming consistency

---

## 4.2 Mandatory Group Members

⚠️ All members must be added before sending any message.

1. Customer KNITI Responsible Person  
2. Customer Support Team Lead  
3. Sales Representative  
4. CTO  
5. Operations Manager  
6. Service & Validation Team Manager/PM  
7. Software Team Manager/PM  
8. Validation Team  

### Verification Checklist

- [ ] All 8 members added  
- [ ] All members accepted invitation  
- [ ] No members left group  
- [ ] Member list verified

# 5. Communication Standards

## 5.1 Professional Conduct

- Maintain professional tone
- Avoid slang or informal language
- Use clear and structured communication

## 5.2 Message Standards

- Include timestamps
- Mention affected components
- Provide status, actions taken, next steps

## 5.3 Prohibited Activities

- ❌ Personal messages  
- ❌ Off-topic discussions  
- ❌ Confidential information sharing  
- ❌ Promotional content  
- ❌ Non-urgent messages outside business hours  

---

## 5.4 Escalation Protocol

For critical issues:

- Tag: `@Operations Manager` or `@Service Lead`
- Mention severity (Critical / High / Medium / Low)
- Provide impact summary
- Specify required action and timeline

---

## 5.5 Response Time SLA

| Priority | Response Time |
|----------|--------------|
| Critical | Within 1 hour |
| High | Within 4 hours |
| Medium | Within 24 hours |
| Low | Within 48 hours |

---

# 8. Troubleshooting

### Unable to Create Group
Verify WhatsApp status, storage, phone number registration.

### Members Not Accepting
Verify numbers, resend invite, confirm WhatsApp installation.

### Message Sent Before All Members Added
Resend full introduction sequence after confirmation.

### Member Leaves Group
Re-add immediately and inform Operations Manager.
