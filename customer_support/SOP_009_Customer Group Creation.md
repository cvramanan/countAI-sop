# WhatsApp Group Creation SOP (KNITI Installation)

---

## 1. Objective

To ensure proper communication, coordination, and structured support after KNITI product installation by creating an official WhatsApp group including all responsible stakeholders.

---

## 2. Scope

This SOP applies to all new **KNITI installations** at customer locations.

---

## 3. Responsibility

The **KNITI Installation Engineer** is responsible for:

- Creating the WhatsApp group immediately after installation
- Adding all mandatory members
- Sending installation confirmation message
- Introducing the Customer Support team
- Introducing the KNITI responsible person

---

## 4. WhatsApp Group Creation Procedure

### 4.1 Group Name Format

The group name must follow the below format: Customer Company Name – KNITI


**Example:**

> ABC Textiles – KNITI

---

### 4.2 Mandatory Group Members

The following members must be added to the group:

1. Customer KNITI Responsible Person
2. Customer Support Team
3. Sales Person
4. CTO
5. Operations Manager
6. Service & Validation Team Manager & PM
7. Software Team Manager & PM
8. Validation Team

⚠️ No group shall be created without the above members.

---

### 4.3 Installation Confirmation Message

After successful installation, the Installation Engineer must send the following message:

> Dear Sir,  
> The KNITI product has been successfully installed.  
> The system is successfully capturing the committed defects.  
> Thank you for your cooperation and great support.

---

### 4.4 Customer Support Introduction Message

After installation confirmation, introduce the Customer Support team:

> This is our Customer Support Team.@
> If you have any questions or require any support, please feel free to contact us.  
> We are happy to assist you. Thank you.

---

### 4.5 KNITI Responsible Person Introduction

Then introduce the KNITI responsible person by tagging them:

> This is our KNITI responsible person @Name.  
> If you need any support or would like to provide feedback, kindly discuss with him.

---

## 5. Communication Guidelines

- All communication must remain professional and official.
- Issue-related discussions must be clear and properly documented.
- No unrelated or personal messages are allowed in the group.
- For major issues or escalations, the concerned manager must be tagged immediately.

---

## 6. Record Maintenance

- The group creation date must be recorded in the installation report.
- The Installation Engineer must confirm WhatsApp group creation in the installation checklist.
