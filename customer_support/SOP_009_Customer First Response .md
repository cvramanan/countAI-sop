# Customer First Response SOP

---

## 1. Objective

To ensure timely customer communication, proper issue tracking, and transparent status updates until issue closure.

---

## 2. Scope

This SOP applies to all customer issues, tickets, and support requests related to the **KNITI product**.

---

## 3. Responsibilities

**Customer Support Executive is responsible for:**

- Acknowledging customer issues
- Providing daily status updates
- Updating completion and target dates in the ticket system

---

## 4. Procedure

### 4.1 Customer Acknowledgement

- All customer issues must be acknowledged within **2 hours** of receiving the request.
- Acknowledgement can be sent via:
  - Email
  - WhatsApp
  - Ticketing system

**Example acknowledgement:**

> We have received your issue and our team is working on it.  
> We will keep you updated on the progress.

---

### 4.2 Daily Status Update

- Issue status must be updated **at least once per day**.
- Update must be shared with the customer **before end of the working day**.
- Update should clearly mention:
  - Current issue status
  - Action taken
  - Next steps

---

### 4.3 Issue Completion Update

Once the issue is resolved:

- Mark the issue as **Completed** in the tracking system.
- Update the **exact completion date**.

If the resolution timeline is extended:

- Update the **revised target date**
- Inform the customer with the reason for delay.

---

## 5. SLA Summary

| Activity                 | Timeline           |
|--------------------------|-------------------|
| Customer acknowledgement | Within 2 hours     |
| Status update            | Daily (End of day) |
| Completion update        | Immediately after resolution |
