# CUSTOMER FIRST RESPONSE SOP
**KNITI Product Support**

**Version:** 2.0  
**Effective Date:** March 2026  

---

# 1. OBJECTIVE

To ensure timely customer communication, proper issue tracking, transparent status updates, and consistent issue resolution for all KNITI product support requests until closure.

---

# 2. SCOPE

This SOP applies to:

- All customer issues, tickets, and support requests related to the **KNITI product**
- All communication channels: **WhatsApp, Email, and Ticketing system**
- Company working days: **Monday to Saturday**

---

# 3. RESPONSIBILITIES

## 3.1 Customer Support Executive

- Acknowledge customer issues within **2 hours** of receipt (during working hours)
- Provide **daily status updates** on all open issues
- Update and track completion dates in the **ticket system with timestamps**
- Log all customer communications in the **ticket system**
- Escalate issues exceeding target dates to **management**

## 3.2 Team Lead / Management

- Review escalated issues (**exceeding target date by 3+ days**)
- Monitor **SLA compliance weekly**
- Approve **priority status changes** for delayed issues

---

# 4. PROCEDURE

## 4.1 Working Days and Business Hours

| Item | Details |
|-----|-----|
| Company Working Days | Monday to Saturday (5.5 days per week) |
| Business Hours | 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM IST |
| Non-Working Day | Sunday |

**Note:** No SLA obligations on Sunday. Issues acknowledged on next Monday.

---

## 4.2 Customer Acknowledgement

**Definition:**  
First message sent to customer confirming issue receipt.

**Timeline:**  
Within **2 hours of issue receipt** (9 AM – 6 PM IST).

**Issues Received on Sunday:**  
Acknowledged by **11:00 AM IST on Monday**

---

### 4.2.1 Acknowledgement Methods (Priority Order)

1. **WhatsApp** – Primary channel (fastest response)
2. **Email** – Secondary channel
3. **Ticketing System** – Tertiary channel

> Use customer's preferred communication method if specified.

---

### 4.2.2 Acknowledgement Message Template

```
We have received your issue [Ticket ID]. Our team is investigating this and will keep you updated daily on the progress. We appreciate your patience.
```

---

## 4.3 Daily Status Updates

| Item | Requirement |
|-----|-----|
| Timing | Before **6:00 PM IST** |
| Frequency | **Minimum once per day** |
| Working Days | Monday to Saturday |

No updates required after issue status becomes **Completed**.

---

### 4.3.1 Status Update Content Requirements

- **Status:** Whether deadline will be met or missed
- **Reason for Delay:** If target date will be exceeded
- **Next Steps:** Planned action
- **Escalation:** If issue exceeds target date by **3+ days**

---

### 4.3.2 Status Update Documentation

All updates must be logged in the **Ticketing System**.

**Format**

```
[Date & Time] Customer Name | Channel Used | Status Message
```

**Timestamp Format**

```
YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM:SS IST
```

**Evidence Required**

- WhatsApp screenshot
- Email copy
- Ticket comment entry

---

### 4.3.3 Status Update Example

```
Date: 2026-03-05 17:30:00 IST
Channel: WhatsApp

Status: WILL MEET DEADLINE.

Our team has completed initial diagnostics.
Next Step: Running final verification tests.
Expected resolution: March 6, 2026.
```

---

# 4.4 Issue Resolution Confirmation

## 4.4.1 Definition of Resolution

Issue is **RESOLVED** only when both conditions are met:

1. **Internal Verification** – Support team confirms issue is fixed
2. **Customer Confirmation** – Customer confirms resolution

---

## 4.4.2 Issue Completion Process

- Record **exact completion date/time**
- Change ticket status to **Completed**
- Send final confirmation to customer **within 1 hour**
- Log resolution details in ticket
- Archive ticket

---

### 4.4.3 Completion Notification Template

```
Your issue [Ticket ID] has been successfully resolved.
If you experience any further issues, please contact us immediately.
Thank you for your patience.
```

---

# 4.5 Target Date Extensions & Delays

## 4.5.1 Timeline Extension

If issue cannot be resolved within target date:

- Update **revised target date**
- Inform customer **before 6:00 PM IST**
- CSE can approve revised dates

---

## 4.5.2 Escalation Protocol (3+ Days Overdue)

If issue exceeds original target date by **3+ days**:

- Ticket marked **Escalated**
- Flagged for **Team Lead / Management review**
- Customer informed of escalation
- Management provides **daily updates**

---

### 4.5.3 Delay Communication Template

```
We apologize for the delay in resolving your issue [Ticket ID].
Due to [REASON], we have extended the completion date to [NEW DATE].
We are prioritizing this issue and will resolve it by then.
```

# 5. SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT (SLA)

## 5.1 Customer Acknowledgement SLA

| Activity | Timeline | Working Days | Escalation |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| Customer Acknowledgement | Within 2 hours | Mon–Sat (9 AM – 6 PM) | Missed → Escalate |

---

## 5.2 Daily Status Update SLA

| Activity | Timing | Scope |
|-----|-----|-----|
| Daily Status Update | Before 6:00 PM IST | Monday – Saturday |

---

## 5.3 Issue Completion SLA

| Activity | Timing | Requirement |
|-----|-----|-----|
| Issue Resolution | Immediately upon resolution | Internal verification + customer confirmation |

---

# 6. KEY DEFINITIONS

| Term | Definition |
|-----|-----|
| Acknowledged | Customer notified issue is received |
| Target Date | Original expected resolution date |
| Revised Target Date | Updated timeline |
| Escalated Status | Issue overdue by 3+ days |
| Resolution | Fix verified internally and by customer |

---

# 7. COMMUNICATION CHANNEL GUIDELINES

## WhatsApp (Primary)

- Immediate acknowledgements
- Quick status updates
- Screenshot evidence stored in ticket

---

## Email (Secondary)

- Detailed communication
- Formal documentation
- Auto logging into ticket system

---

## Ticketing System (Tertiary)

- Internal tracking
- Audit trail
- Mandatory logging of all communications

---

# 8. SUNDAY ISSUE HANDLING

Issues received on **Sunday**:

- No SLA obligations
- Auto reply allowed

Example:

```
We have received your issue.
Our team will acknowledge this by 11:00 AM on Monday.
```

Formal acknowledgement must be sent by **Monday 11:00 AM IST**.

---

# 9. QUALITY ASSURANCE & COMPLIANCE

| Item | Description |
|-----|-----|
| SLA Audits | 10% random tickets reviewed weekly |
| Metrics | 2-hour acknowledgement, daily updates |
| Non-Compliance | Escalation + process review |
| Documentation | Complete timestamp logs required |

---

# 10. EXCEPTIONS & SPECIAL CASES

**Customer Unavailability**

If customer unreachable for **24+ hours**, SLA clock paused.

**External Dependencies**

If issue depends on third-party vendors, timeline updated and communicated.

**System Issues**

If company systems fail, SLA clock paused until restoration.
